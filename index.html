常见问题

如何使用读图？
打开应用后，点击相册按钮选择图片，或点击相机按钮拍摄新照片。应用会自动识别图片中的文字，然后点击播放按钮即可听取语音朗读。
支持哪些语言的识别？
读图支持简体中文、繁体中文和英文的识别，也支持中英文混合的文本识别。
需要联网使用吗？
不需要。读图的所有功能都在设备本地完成，无需网络连接即可使用。这也意味着您的隐私得到了最好的保护。
如何调节语音设置？
点击「语音参数」区域可以展开设置面板，您可以自由调节语速、音调和音量，找到最适合您的朗读效果。
可以后台播放吗？
可以。读图支持后台播放和锁屏播放，您可以在收听语音的同时使用其他应用，或者锁定屏幕继续收听。
如何使用循环播放？
在语音控制区域，开启「循环播放」开关即可。这个功能非常适合学习和记忆场景，可以反复收听同一段文字。
如何暂停和恢复朗读？
在朗读过程中，点击暂停按钮即可暂停。应用会记住当前朗读的位置，再次点击继续按钮，将从暂停的位置继续朗读，无需重新开始。

FAQ

How do I use ReadImg?
Open the app, tap the gallery button to select an image, or tap the camera button to take a new photo. The app will automatically recognize text in the image. Then tap the play button to hear it read aloud.
What languages are supported?
ReadImg supports Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and English recognition, as well as mixed Chinese-English text.
Does it require internet?
No. All features work completely offline on your device. This also means your privacy is fully protected.
How do I adjust voice settings?
Tap the "Voice Settings" area to expand the settings panel. You can freely adjust speech rate, pitch, and volume to find your preferred reading experience.
Can it play in the background?
Yes. ReadImg supports background and lock screen playback. You can listen while using other apps or with the screen locked.
How do I use loop playback?
In the voice control area, turn on the "Loop" switch. This feature is great for learning and memorization, allowing you to repeatedly listen to the same text.
How do I pause and resume?
During playback, tap the pause button to pause. The app remembers your position, and when you tap resume, it continues from where you paused without starting over.
